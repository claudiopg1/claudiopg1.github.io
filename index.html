I am Claudio Pérez, a physicist and current MSc student in Theoretical Physics at Universidad Arturo Prat, Iquique, Chile. My research lies at the intersection of high energy physics and gravitation.

I am particularly interested in the asymptotic structure of fields in general relativity, gauge symmetries at infinity, and the duality between scalar fields and two-form fields. These topics connect classical theoretical physics with deep questions about the nature of spacetime.

research
Scalar Field Asymptotic Structure and the Dual Two Form
Study of the asymptotic structure of a scalar field in gravity and its relationship to the dual two-form field. The analysis addresses boundary conditions at null infinity, the associated conserved charges, and the symmetries that emerge in the asymptotic limit — connecting with the BMS symmetry program and its generalizations.
